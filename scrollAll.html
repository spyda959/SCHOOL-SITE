About Our School

Brief History

King David Montessori School came into existence in 2015, starting out with only two children and a single caregiver. Over the years the school has carved a niche for itself as one of the well managed private educational institutions in the Sekondi-Takoradi metropolis in the Western Region of Ghana. King David Montessori School is registered as a christian school. The management team is headed by Rev. Alex Koufie-Monney the founder and proprieter and Mrs. Sabina Koufie-Monney the principal Today, King David Montessori School can boasts of sixteen (16) teaching staff and six (6) non teaching staff. In terms of infrastructure the school has ten (10) classrooms available now with kitchen, offices and dining hall. Three planned storey building which includes classrooms, administration block, I.C.T laboratory, kitchen, dinin and playing grounds under construction. King David Montessori admits students of any race, colour, national and ethinic origin to all the rights and privileges programs and activities generally accorded or made available to students at the school. Admission is available from Creche up to Grade six (6). The school has established a reputation for providing high standards and expectations within a well ordered and caring environment. It aims to encourage pupils to achieve their best and to lay foundations on which a brighter future is built.

Academics

**Academics at Our School** At King David Montessori, we are dedicated to fostering academic excellence through a well-rounded curriculum that challenges and inspires students. Our programs are designed to cater to diverse learning styles and encourage critical thinking, creativity, and collaboration. From early childhood education to advanced educational level, we ensure that every student receives personalized attention from qualified, passionate teachers with degrees. We offer all aproved subjects by the GES, preparing students for both academic success and real-world challenges. With modern facilities, interactive learning environments, and a commitment to continuous improvement, our goal is to help every student reach their full potential.

Admission

**Admissions at Our School** At King David Montessori, we welcome students who are eager to learn, grow, and contribute to our vibrant community. Our admissions process is designed to be straightforward and transparent, ensuring that every prospective student and family feels supported. We offer rolling admissions, allowing applications year-round, and we encourage families to begin the process early. Our admissions team is available to assist with any questions and guide you through each step, from the initial inquiry to enrollment. The application process includes submitting required documents, meeting our dedicated staff, and visiting the campus to get a feel for our welcoming environment.
